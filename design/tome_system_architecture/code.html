The Astral Confluence & Ether Mechanics

A comprehensive study of planar intersections and their residual energy signatures recorded during the last cycle.

The Astral Confluence

The convergence of the ethereal planes, commonly referred to as the Astral Confluence, represents a unique period of metaphysical instability. During this time, the barriers separating the physical realm from the latent energy fields of the Aether grow exceedingly thin, allowing for phenomena previously considered theoretical.

Observations from the High Observatory indicate that these confluences operate on an elliptical cycle, roughly every four hundred solar years. The implications for arcano-mechanics are profound.

"To gaze into the confluence is to see the gears of reality laid bare, unshielded by the mundane laws of physics." — Seeker Valerius

Mechanics of Ether

Ether is not a substance, but a state of resonance. It acts as the conductive medium through which arcane intent translates into physical reality. Understanding its mechanics requires a paradigm shift from classical thermodynamics.

Resonance Frequencies

Different forms of energy possess distinct resonant frequencies when passing through the Ether. These can be quantified and, theoretically, manipulated.

  • Alpha Frequencies: High volatility, associated with thermal and kinetic expressions.
  • Beta Frequencies: Stable, enduring states, used in structural abjuration and containment fields.
  • Gamma Frequencies: Highly theoretical, interacting directly with chronal and spatial dimensions.

Decay Rates

Arcane constructs inevitably degrade. The decay rate of an etheric structure is inversely proportional to the precision of its initial resonance tuning. The standard formula for calculating etheric decay is:

D(t) = E_0 * e^(-λt) * (1 - R_tuning)

Where E_0 is initial energy, λ is the planar resistance constant, and R_tuning is the resonance alignment coefficient.

Historical Anomalies

Records predating the Cataclysm hint at constructs that completely bypassed standard decay rates. These "perpetual engines" suggest a mastery over Gamma frequencies that modern archivists have yet to replicate.

Further investigation into the deeply buried archives of the Sanctum is required to uncover the lost methodologies of these ancient artificers.
